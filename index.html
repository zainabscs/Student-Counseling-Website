Money-Saving Tips

  • Work part-time (20 hrs/week) to earn NZ$300-400/week
  • Choose shared accommodation to save on housing costs
  • Cook at home instead of eating out frequently
  • Use student discounts for transport and entertainment
  • Apply for scholarships and financial aid programs
  • Consider smaller cities for lower living costs
